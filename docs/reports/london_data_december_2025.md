# London Data Roles Hiring Market
## December 2025

---

## Executive Summary

**1,863** direct employer job postings analyzed

The London data market is **highly fragmented** with 689 employers hiring—no single company dominates. ML and AI roles now account for nearly **1 in 4** openings, while senior-level positions make up over half of all demand.

**Key findings:**
- ML Engineer is the #1 role (21%), followed closely by Data Engineer (21%)
- 55% of roles require senior-level experience
- 92% of positions are Individual Contributor track
- Enterprise companies drive over half of hiring (52%)

---

455 recruitment agency listings identified and excluded (20% of raw postings)

## Key Takeaways: Job Seekers

- **Target ML and engineering roles** — ML Engineer and Data Engineer together represent 41% of openings

- **Hybrid is the norm** — Where disclosed, 2 in 3 roles offer hybrid arrangements
- **Spread your net wide** — Top 15 employers represent just 9% of market; don't fixate on big names

## Methodology

This report analyzes **1,863** direct employer job postings for Data roles in London during December 2025.

**Data sources:** Aggregated from major job boards and company applicant tracking systems.

**Data quality measures:**
- Agency filtering: 455 recruitment agency postings (20%) identified and excluded
- Deduplication: Jobs reposted within 30 days consolidated
- Classification: LLM-powered taxonomy for subfamily, seniority, skills, and working arrangement

**Limitations:**
- Some roles posted exclusively on company career sites may not be captured
- Skills analysis limited to 165 roles with full job descriptions
- Salary data unavailable (no UK pay transparency legislation)
- Working arrangement specified in 38% of postings
